About

Mark Richard Robinson

I am Mark Richard Robinson. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website. I am a minister, writer, artist, and public theologian. Much of my work begins with ordinary life: the memories we hold, the hope we inhabit, and the struggles we face. It is rooted in scripture, justice, liberation, healing, and the dignity of human community.

I am drawn to theology that speaks truthfully about life in its struggle and joy, while still daring to hope in the future God is bringing into being..

I serve congregations within the United Reformed Church in the United Kingdom and am the author of A Measure of Breath
