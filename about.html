About

Mark Richard Robinson

Mark Richard Robinson is a minister, writer, artist, and public theologian whose work explores hope, justice, breath, faith, and the presence of God in ordinary life.

His ministry is shaped by concerns for justice, liberation, mercy, healing, and the dignity of human community. His writing moves through scripture, memory, embodiment, struggle, and the future God is bringing into being.

Through sermons, essays, visual art, and published work, Mark reflects on faith as something lived in the body, carried in community, and shaped within the realities of human experience.

He serves congregations within the United Reformed Church in the United Kingdom and is the author of A Measure of Breath.
